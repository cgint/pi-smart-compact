- **[Prompt-Only First Milestone]**: The project prioritizes a prompt-based solution over side-storage systems to minimize complexity and latency.

- **[Compaction Strategy]**: The compaction logic should preserve "Work Stream Evolution" (trajectory of changes) rather than just current state, based on trace-v2 findings.
